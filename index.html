About Me

I am a Computer Engineering student with a strong interest in software development, embedded systems, and low-level programming. I enjoy understanding how systems work at a fundamental level and building solutions that are both efficient and meaningful.

Academically, my focus areas include software development, database systems, and systems design. I find satisfaction in tackling complex problems and turning them into working, practical applications through code.

Beyond engineering, I have a strong personal side — I love travelling, meeting new people, and documenting life through photography. These experiences shape how I think, communicate, and approach challenges creatively.

I participated in an Erasmus+ exchange program in Germany, which is one of the most valuable experiences of my life. It expanded my perspective, improved my independence, and helped me grow both academically and personally. This portfolio reflects where I am now — and where I am heading.
